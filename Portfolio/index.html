About me

UX, UI, Digital & vanilla web designer. I have a master in Interaction Design and Technologies from Chalmers University of Technology and a bachelors degree in Cognitive Science from Gothenburg University.


I look for talented and teams to join who work creatively and that are solution oriented. As a team player I am loyal and supportive and fully engaged. As a designer I center my work around empathy, with the intention to create better products for a better future, reaching my objectives by balancing empathizing with users, creative exploration of the solution space and research-based knowledge.


If you are interested to get to know me better, don't hestitate to reach out and let's explore what we can do together.
